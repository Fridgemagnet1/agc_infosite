Our Brands

AGC International is the authorised importer and distributor for the following brands across Central Asia and Europe.

R

Rippleco Inhalers

Nicotine-Free · Tobacco-Free · Organic

Rippleco produces a range of personal inhalers made entirely from organic ingredients — completely free from nicotine and tobacco. Designed as a healthier alternative to smoking, Rippleco Inhalers give consumers the familiar ritual of inhaling without any of the harmful substances associated with traditional cigarettes.

For consumers looking to reduce or eliminate their dependence on nicotine, or simply seeking a clean, organic inhaling experience, Rippleco represents a genuinely new kind of product in the market.

NP

Nicotine Pouches

Smokeless · Spit-Free · Tobacco-Free

AGC International distributes a curated selection of leading nicotine pouch brands — small, discreet pouches that sit under the lip and deliver nicotine without any smoke, vapour, or spitting required. All brands in our portfolio are tobacco-free, using pharmaceutical-grade nicotine instead.

Nicotine pouches have seen rapid growth globally as consumers seek modern, socially acceptable alternatives to cigarettes and traditional snus. Our portfolio includes a variety of strengths and flavours to suit different preferences, sourced from trusted manufacturers across Europe and Asia.
