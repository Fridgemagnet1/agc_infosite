About AGC International

A global trading company built on strong partnerships and quality products.

Who We Are

AGC International is an international trading and distribution company with a current focus on importing fast-moving consumer goods into Mongolia. We work with a network of established distribution channels to ensure products reach consumers efficiently and reliably.

Our operations are backed by strong trade relationships across Asia-Pacific — particularly in Japan and South Korea — giving us access to premium products and trusted supply chains.

Our Mission

To connect international markets with quality consumer goods — bringing innovative, accessible products to underserved markets through reliable logistics and trusted partnerships.

Our Products

Rippleco Inhalers

A nicotine-free and tobacco-free alternative made from organic ingredients. Rippleco Inhalers offer a clean, modern experience for consumers looking to move away from traditional smoking products without sacrificing habit or ritual.

Nicotine Pouches

Small, smokeless, and spit-free — our range of nicotine pouch brands are tobacco-free and designed for discreet, convenient use anywhere. A modern nicotine solution for today's on-the-go consumer.

Wider FMCG Range

Beyond our headline brands, AGC International distributes a broader range of fast-moving consumer goods sourced from our Asia-Pacific network, meeting diverse demand across the Mongolian market.

Our Reach

With roots across Asia-Pacific and an operational focus on Mongolia, AGC International bridges the gap between international suppliers and local markets. Our connections in Japan and South Korea allow us to source quality products and maintain the supply chain integrity our distribution partners depend on.

Our Values

Quality First

We only bring products to market that meet a high standard — for our partners and for end consumers.

Partnership

Long-term relationships with suppliers, distributors, and clients are at the core of how we operate.

Innovation

We actively seek out modern alternatives and emerging product categories that serve evolving consumer needs.
