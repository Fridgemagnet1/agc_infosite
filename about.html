About Us

Background

AGC International is a global trading company focused on importing and distributing fast-moving consumer goods across Central Asia and Europe. We operate through established networks of distribution channels, connecting quality international brands with a rapidly evolving consumer market.

Brands

AGC International is the authorised importer and distributor for a growing portfolio of brands across Central Asia and Europe — including Rippleco, a range of leading nicotine pouch brands, and further consumer goods across our wider FMCG network. Visit our Brands page to learn more.

Mission

To provide consumers with modern, healthier alternatives to traditional tobacco products — and to deliver innovative consumer goods to growing markets through trusted partnerships and reliable distribution.

Values

Quality, Partnership, Innovation & Integrity.
