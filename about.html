About Us

Background

AGC International is a global trading company focused on importing and distributing fast-moving consumer goods across Mongolia. We operate through established networks of distribution channels, connecting quality international brands with a rapidly evolving consumer market.

Our focus is rooted in a simple but important shift happening worldwide — consumers are moving away from traditional cigarettes and tobacco products in search of healthier, cleaner alternatives. We recognised this change early and positioned ourselves to meet that demand, bringing modern, better-for-you products into markets where access to these alternatives has historically been limited.

Beyond health-conscious alternatives, we actively seek out products that fit the direction markets are heading — smokeless, discreet, convenient, and effective. Our strong trade relationships in Japan and South Korea allow us to source innovative products and maintain the supply chain reliability our distribution partners depend on.

Brands

AGC International is the authorised importer and distributor for a growing portfolio of brands in Mongolia — including Rippleco, a range of leading nicotine pouch brands, and further consumer goods across our wider FMCG network. Visit our Brands page to learn more.

Mission

To provide consumers with modern, healthier alternatives to traditional tobacco products — and to deliver innovative consumer goods to growing markets through trusted partnerships and reliable distribution.

Vision

To become a leading distributor of better-for-you consumer products across Mongolia and the broader Asia-Pacific region, built on years of strong supplier and partner relationships.

Values

Quality, Partnership, Innovation & Integrity.
